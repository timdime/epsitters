About EP Sitters

EP Sitters was founded by four responsible and fun-loving 6th grade girls from the Edison Park neighborhood in Chicago. Each of them has younger siblings and knows what it takes to care for and entertain kids of all ages. They provide babysitting services in Edison Park, Park Ridge, and Niles. Parents love the peace of mind that comes with local, trusted sitters who are part of their community.
